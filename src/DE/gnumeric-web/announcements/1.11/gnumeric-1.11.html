The New Development Gnumeric 1.11 Series is Now Available.

The Gnumeric Team is proud to announce the 1.11.x series. This is the product of almost two years of development.

What's New?

  • Gnumeric uses GTK+ 3, not GTK+ 2.
  • Gnumeric uses GTK+'s ui manager files, not libglade.
  • Gnumeric uses GSettings, not GConf.
  • Lots of small bug fixes.

Requirements

Gnumeric 1.11.x requires the new Goffice 0.9.x library series, a fairly recent libgsf, and a GTK+ stack. See here for details.
